The survey data confirms that the event's audience has a significant international dimension. In summary, the core attendee is a highly specialised professional with a strong focus on digital and crime scene forensics, operating within a global community but with a significant concentration in London and the South East of England.

The strategic value of our event is directly proportional to how well it meets the core professional needs of its audience. The survey data reveals that the audience is primarily motivated by the need for continuous learning and professional connection. The top three reasons cited for attending events are:

In conclusion, the target attendee is driven overwhelmingly by the need for education, professional development, and peer-to-peer networking. This knowledge provides a clear mandate for how Forensics Europe Expo 2026 can be structured to meet these fundamental needs.
